6-Channel Performance Summary

  • In-Store (64% revenue): Core foundation, stable growth, highest foot traffic
  • Online (18% revenue): Fastest growing traditional channel (↑30% YoY)
  • Social Commerce (8% revenue): Emerging powerhouse (↑50% YoY, TikTok Shop leading)
  • Wholesale (6% revenue): Highest ATV (฿45K), stable B2B channel
  • Pop-up/Events (3% revenue): Seasonal, festival-driven, high engagement
  • Partnership (1% revenue): Third-party leverage, distributor network

Strategic Recommendations

  • Accelerate Social Commerce: 50% YoY growth indicates high potential, invest in TikTok/Instagram channels
  • Maintain In-Store foundation: 64% core business, optimize rather than expand
  • Scale Online infrastructure: 30% growth requires logistics, tech, and fulfillment upgrades
  • Wholesale stability: Maintain relationships, predictable revenue stream
  • Bangkok dominance: 44% of stores, focus expansion on North/Northeast regions
  • New store performance: 15 stores opened in 2024+, monitor maturation curve

2025 Growth Projections

  • Overall revenue growth: 25-30% YoY (driven by omnichannel expansion)
  • Social Commerce: Expected to reach 12% revenue share (↑50% from current)
  • Online: Target 22% revenue share (logistics and platform improvements)
  • In-Store: Maintain 60-62% share (optimize existing locations)
  • Regional expansion: Add 8-10 stores in underserved North/Northeast markets
